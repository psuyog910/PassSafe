Welcome to SafeX

SafeX is where your password security meets simplicity! We ensure that your passwords are securely encrypted and can only be decrypted with your personal passkey, which we do not store. This means that your data remains safe and accessible only by you.

Your username and password while logging in are secured using the SHA256 encryption algorithm in the backend, so you can rest assured about the security of your credentials.
